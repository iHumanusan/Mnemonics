
| No. | Japanese | English Meaning | Mnemonics |
|-----|-----------|-----------------|------------|
| 1 | かんがえます | to think, consider | “I need to think like a kangaroo.” Kangae sounds like kangaroo. |
| 2 | つきます | to arrive | “The moon (tsuki) arrives every night.” Tsukimasu → arrive. |
| 3 | とります（としを〜） | to get older, age | “Time takes your years.” Toru → to take. |
| 4 | たります | to be enough, suffice | “Does it fill the tar?” Tarimasu → enough. |
| 5 | いなか | countryside, rural | “In a car… you reach the countryside.” Inaka → in a car. |
| 6 | チャンス | chance, opportunity | Loanword — chance. |
| 7 | おく | to put, place | “OK, put it there.” Oku → OK-put. |
| 8 | もし〜たら | if, if ~ happens | “Moshi moshi… if you can hear me?” Moshi = if, tara = then. |
| 9 | いみ | meaning | “The meaning is in me.” Imi → in me. |
