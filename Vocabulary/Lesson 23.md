
| No. | Japanese | English Meaning | Mnemonics |
|-----|-----------|-----------------|------------|
| 1 | ききます | to ask | Kiki = to hear and also to ask. |
| 2 | まわします | to turn, rotate | “Ma-wa-shi → my washing machine turns.” Think of a washing machine rotating. |
| 3 | ひきます | to pull, play (string instrument) | “He keeps pulling the guitar strings.” Hi-ki → he keeps → pulling/playing. |
| 4 | かえます | to change | Kaeru = change. Change the rope (kayiri). |
| 5 | さわります | to touch | “Sawari — touching.” |
| 6 | でます（おつりが〜） | (change) comes out | “De machine gives me money that comes out — de-masu.” Change comes out of a machine. |
| 7 | あるきます | to walk | ARU (around) + KI (go) → walk. |
| 8 | わたります | to cross | Watari sounds like “water-y” → cross a bridge over water. |
| 9 | まがります | to turn (direction) | “Ma-gar → make a turn.” |
| 10 | さびしい | lonely | “Sad be she.” Sabishii → lonely. |
| 11 | おゆ | hot water | “Oh you only shower in hot water.” |
| 12 | おと | sound | — |
| 13 | サイズ | size | — |
| 14 | こしょう | breakdown, malfunction | “Car-show is broken.” |
| 15 | みち | road | “My chi walks on the road.” |
| 16 | こうさてん | intersection | “Cars at ten directions meet.” |
| 17 | しんごう | traffic light | “Shine-go!” Lights shine, then say go. |
| 18 | かど | corner | “Cardboard corner.” Ka-do → card → corner. |
| 19 | はし | bridge | Means both chopsticks and bridge. |
| 20 | ちゅうしゃじょう | parking lot | “Choose a joker in parking lot.” |
| 21 | たてもの | building | “Tate built the building.” |
| 22 | なんかいも | many times | “N number of times.” Nan = how many, kai = times. |
| 23 | ーめ | the -th (order) | Suffix for order: first, second, third, etc. |
